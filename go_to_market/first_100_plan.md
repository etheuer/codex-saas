# First $100 Execution Plan (14 Days)

## Offer
- Product: PodHunt Briefs
- Pricing test: $29/month or $19 first month for first 10 users
- Promise: "Get 10 high-fit podcast targets + personalized outreach in under 10 minutes."

## Customer profile
- Bootstrapped B2B SaaS founders actively doing content-led growth.
- Trigger signal: recently posted about distribution, pipeline, or founder-led marketing.

## Channel strategy
1. **Direct outreach (primary):** 50 handpicked founders on X/LinkedIn.
2. **Communities (secondary):** Indie Hackers, relevant Slack groups.
3. **Service partner referrals:** Podcast booking freelancers/agencies.

## 14-day action sprint
- Day 1–2: Publish landing copy and demo GIF/screenshot.
- Day 3–7: Send 15 personalized outbound messages/day.
- Day 8–10: Onboard free trial users with concierge setup.
- Day 11–14: Convert to paid with early adopter discount and social proof snippets.

## Outreach script
"Hey {name}, noticed you're pushing founder-led distribution. I built PodHunt Briefs to cut podcast prospecting from hours to minutes by ranking podcast fit + drafting outreach. Want me to run your SaaS through it and send 5 best targets?"

## Revenue math to first $100
- Goal: 4 customers x $29 = $116 MRR.
- Conservative funnel:
  - 200 outbound touches
  - 20 replies (10%)
  - 8 trials (40% of replies)
  - 4 paid (50% of trials)
